I started to show interest in websites when I was 12, just for fun. Some years later, I worked on my first UI project. In 2020 I moved to Denmark to study Multimedia design, and work on UI/UX projects by sharing my passion with other like-minded people.

Before studies in Demmark, I didn't know much about user research and UX overall, which was one of the reasons to study Multimedia design - learn more about what happens before jumping into the visuals. As a Multimedia design student I had ability to learn about UX and everything around it as well as use it in real projects.
